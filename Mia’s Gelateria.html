Mia’s Gelateria

36 Water St, New Shoreham
12:00 pm - 10:00 pm

Quick bite. It’s the Italian version of ice cream with the major difference being that it’s made with milk instead of cream. This means less air and a richer flavor in each bite. Available in 18 flavors.
